1. Introduction

ProcAI Corporation is seeking proposals for the implementation of a comprehensive Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) system to streamline business processes, improve data management, and enhance operational efficiency across all departments. This document outlines the requirements, evaluation criteria, and submission guidelines for potential vendors.

2. Company Background

ProcAI Corporation is a leading provider of procurement solutions with 500 employees across 5 locations in the United States. Founded in 2010, the company has experienced significant growth over the past decade and now requires a modern ERP system to replace its legacy systems and support continued expansion.

3. Project Objectives

The primary objectives of this ERP implementation project are:

  • Replace multiple legacy systems with a single, integrated ERP solution
  • Streamline business processes and improve operational efficiency
  • Enhance data visibility and reporting capabilities
  • Improve collaboration across departments
  • Support scalability for future growth
  • Reduce manual processes and increase automation

4. Scope of Work

The selected vendor will be responsible for the following:

  • ERP software licensing and implementation
  • System configuration and customization
  • Data migration from legacy systems
  • Integration with existing systems
  • User training and knowledge transfer
  • Post-implementation support and maintenance

5. Functional Requirements

5.1 Financial Management

  • General ledger
  • Accounts payable and receivable
  • Budgeting and forecasting
  • Financial reporting and analytics
  • Multi-currency support
  • Tax management

5.2 Human Resources

  • Employee management
  • Payroll processing
  • Benefits administration
  • Performance evaluation
  • Recruitment and onboarding
  • Time and attendance tracking

5.3 Supply Chain Management

  • Procurement and purchasing
  • Inventory management
  • Supplier relationship management
  • Demand forecasting
  • Warehouse management

5.4 Customer Relationship Management

  • Contact management
  • Sales tracking
  • Customer service
  • Marketing campaign management
  • Customer analytics

5.5 Technical Requirements

  • Cloud-based solution with secure access from anywhere
  • Mobile application for key functions
  • Robust security features and role-based access control
  • Customizable dashboards and reporting
  • API capabilities for integration with other systems
  • Scalability to support business growth

6. Compliance Requirements

  • Data Security: Must comply with NIST 800-53 security controls and include encryption for data at rest and in transit.
  • Accessibility: Must comply with ADA and Section 508 accessibility requirements.
  • Audit Trail: Must maintain a comprehensive audit trail for all transactions and system changes.
  • Data Privacy: Must support compliance with GDPR, CCPA, and other relevant data privacy regulations.
  • Industry Standards: Must adhere to relevant industry standards and best practices.
